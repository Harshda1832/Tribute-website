A P J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, widely celebrated as the "Missile Man of India," was a visionary scientist, dedicated educator, and the 11th President of India. His contributions to India's space and defense programs, particularly in the development of ballistic missiles and launch vehicles, earned him national acclaim. Beyond his scientific achievements, Kalam was a fervent advocate for education and youth empowerment, inspiring millions with his vision for a developed India. His presidency (2002-2007) was marked by humility, simplicity, and a deep connection with the people, making him one of the most admired leaders in the nation's history.
